Приветственное слово директора

Об институте Приветствие Статистика в цифрах

Anton Pronin

General Director of the D.I. Mendeleyev Institute for Metrology (VNIIM)

For 180 years, since its founding in 1842, the D.I. Mendeleyev Institute for Metrology has been realizing ideas for progress and technical leadership of Russia based on precise measurements. On top of traditional industries, the Institute’s staff provides accuracy and uniformity of measurements at the forefront of science and technology. We are part of both global processes and practical aspects of digitalization – the transition to fundamentally new technologies in production and consumption.

Every Scientific Department and Laboratory takes pride in solving challenging technical and scientific problems. Thanks to its Measurement Standards and the highest qualification of its specialists, VNIIM has become integral to a broad research and production cooperation. Our partners include the leading enterprises of State Corporations, fuel, transport, and energy distribution companies, organizations in the field of medicine and healthcare, Federal Scientific Centers, etc.

Cooperation with VNIIM in research and production guarantees high quality of products, and competitiveness in Russian and foreign markets.
